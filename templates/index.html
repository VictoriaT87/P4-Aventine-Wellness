Wellness Sessions

Each Wellness session lasts 1 hour and combines both Meditation and Yoga.

All sessions are 1 on 1 in a private setting, allowing for maximum relaxation.

Our sessions are guided by a trained meditation facilitator.

Meditation

Meditation can produce a deep state of relaxation and a tranquil mind. During meditation, you focus your attention and eliminate the stream of jumbled thoughts that may be crowding your mind and causing stress.

Yoga

Yoga is an ancient system of physical, mental and spiritual practices that include breathing techniques, postures, relaxation, chanting, and other meditation methods.

Meet The Instructors

Meet our Wonderful Instructors!

Learn a little about their journies to becoming Yoga Instructors for Aventine.

Karolina

Karolina has been teaching in Ireland and abroad for the past 7 years.

She is certified in Pranayama, Mindfulness and Buddhist Meditation and often incorporates elements of these practices in her classes as a complement to the asana practice.

Vickie

Vickie has been a yoga and wellness enthusiast for over 10 years.

Her methods of teaching are considered patient, fun, energetic and nurturing with focus on breath and slow flow movements. She teaches many styles including Hatha, Yin, Restorative, and Vinyasa.

Sarah

Sarah found Yoga, Meditation and Ayurveda when she experienced anxiety and depression. It has been invaluable to her ever since to help deal with everyday stress and the challenges of life.

With Sarah, you will explore the Philosophy of Yoga. It's this Philosophy that aided her recovery with mental health issues. There are elements of mindfulness & meditation at the beginning and the end.
